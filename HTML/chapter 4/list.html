    making a cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease a cake pan with butter or oil and lightly dust it with flour or use parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  4. In another bowl, beat the butter and sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  6. Add vanilla extract and mix well.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk. Mix until smooth.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and spread it evenly.
  9. Bake for 25–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  11. Transfer the cake to a wire rack and let it cool completely.
  12. Decorate with frosting, icing, or powdered sugar as desired. Slice and serve.

    materials required

  • 1½ cups (180g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • ½ cup (120ml) milk
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
